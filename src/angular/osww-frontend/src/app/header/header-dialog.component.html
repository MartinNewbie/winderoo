Winderoo is resetting

Resetting takes 20 seconds:

  • Reset is complete when the second LED stops blinking, then turns solid.

When the second LED turns solid:

  • Connect your mobile device to the new Wifi network called, Winderoo Setup, and add Winderoo to an existing WiFi network.

Once the config window closes on your mobile device:

  • Your mobile device will disconnect from the Winderoo Setup network.
  • Wait for your mobile device to connect to your existing WiFi network.
  • Now press the physical reset button on Winderoo's circuit board - this reboots Winderoo.

Winderoo is now available at:

  • http://winderoo.local
